A weird recurrence relation

Problem 463

The function f is defined for all positive integers as follows:

  • f(1)=1
  • f(3)=3
  • f(2n)=f(n)
  • f(4n+1)=2f(2n+1)−f(n)
  • f(4n+3)=3f(2n+1)−2f(n)

The function S(n) is defined as ∑i=1nf(i).

S(8)=22 and S(100)=3604.

Find S(337). Give the last 9 digits of your answer.
